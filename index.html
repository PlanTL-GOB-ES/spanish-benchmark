What is EvalES?

The EvalES benchmark consists of 10 tasks: Text Classification (MLDoc), Named Entity Recognition and Classification (NERC), Paraphrase Identification (PAWS-X), Part-of-Speech Tagging (UD-POS), Semantic Textual Similarity (STS), Textual Entailment (XNLI), Question Answering (QA) and Winograd.
